About me

Samir Sapkota, a dedicated Cybersecurity Analyst with a strong commitment to safeguarding digital assets and mitigating cyber threats. Hailing from the beautiful mountainous country Nepal, Samir discovered his passion for the IT sector, specifically in Cybersecurity, while pursuing his Bachelors degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering.

Recognizing the need for professional development in his field, Samir made the decision to relocate to Canada to pursue a postgraduate program in Cybersecurity and Computer Forensics. With a total of 5 years of experience in the IT field, including 3 years as a Cybersecurity Analyst, Samirs expertise and drive for growth led him to join CDW as a Cloud Security Intern. Demonstrating his proficiency and dedication, he was subsequently promoted to the role of MSS Analyst.

Motivated by his passion for continuous improvement and staying abreast of industry advancements, Samir is currently focused on expanding his skill set in DevOps Security. He is proactively acquiring knowledge in cutting-edge technologies such as Docker, Kubernetes, Ansible, Terraform, and Git. By mastering these tools, Samir aims to enhance his capabilities in securing DevOps environments and effectively mitigating emerging cyber threats.

Guided by the famous saying by Lolly Daskal,"Learn to trust the journey, even when you don't understand it", Samir embraces the challenges and uncertainties that come with his professional path, always seeking opportunities for growth and learning.
